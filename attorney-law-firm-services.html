Practical Support for Legal Professionals

Attorneys and law firms frequently need dependable outside support for assignments that are important, time-sensitive, and document-heavy but do not require additional attorney staffing.

Paper Chasers LLC provides project-based assistance for service of process, courthouse delivery, document transport, case-file organization, exhibit preparation, records coordination, factual summaries, and other approved administrative tasks.

Attorney-Directed Research Support

Paper Chasers LLC may provide administrative legal research support when the assignment is directed by a licensed attorney or law firm.

Research assignments may include locating publicly available statutes, regulations, cases, government materials, court information, agency documents, records, and other source materials identified within the attorney’s requested scope.

Results may be organized into source lists, factual research summaries, document collections, chronologies, or other attorney-requested formats.

Source Collection

Locating and organizing publicly available primary and secondary research materials requested by counsel.

Case and Statute Retrieval

Collecting identified cases, statutes, regulations, rules, decisions, or government publications for attorney review.

Research Organization

Creating organized research folders, source tables, citation lists, issue files, or factual summaries.

Research support is administrative and performed under attorney direction. Paper Chasers LLC does not provide independent legal opinions, determine controlling authority, advise clients, or replace an attorney’s legal analysis and professional judgment.

Professional Boundaries

No Attorney-Client Relationship

Paper Chasers LLC does not form an attorney-client relationship with the law firm’s client and does not act as substitute counsel.

No Independent Legal Judgment

We do not independently choose legal strategy, determine claims or defenses, calculate legal deadlines, or decide what should be filed.

Attorney Direction Required

Legal research and litigation-document assignments must remain within the scope and direction established by supervising counsel.

No Guaranteed Outcome

Process service, delivery, filing support, research, or document preparation does not guarantee any legal or procedural result.

Confidential or privileged materials should be clearly identified. Firms are responsible for determining what information may be shared, selecting appropriate transmission methods, and providing any required confidentiality or handling instructions.
