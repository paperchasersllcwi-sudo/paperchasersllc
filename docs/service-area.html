Before Requesting Service

Providing complete information helps us determine whether the assignment can be accepted and how it should be priced.

  • Type of service requested;
  • Exact service or delivery address;
  • County and municipality;
  • Required completion date or deadline;
  • Number and type of documents;
  • Recipient, business, or agency information;
  • Known access or scheduling issues;
  • Required attempts or delivery instructions;
  • Expected proof of completion;
  • Return-document requirements;
  • Client or attorney contact information; and
  • Any special confidentiality or handling requirements.
