Who We Are

Paper Chasers LLC is an Appleton-based Wisconsin business providing professional administrative, litigation-support, process-service, courier, and document-related services.

The company was created to help people move important assignments forward without unnecessary confusion, missed communication, or disorganized paperwork.

Many clients do not need complicated promises. They need someone who answers the phone, confirms the scope of work, explains the next step, keeps records organized, provides meaningful updates, and follows through.

That practical need is the foundation of Paper Chasers LLC.

Our guiding principle: Understand the assignment, define the scope, complete the work carefully, document the result, and communicate clearly.
Michael Winius — Owner, Paper Chasers LLC

Meet the Owner

Paper Chasers LLC is owned and operated by Michael Winius.

Michael built the company around firsthand experience with complicated paperwork, public agencies, legal procedures, deadlines, records, business operations, and the practical challenges people face when they are trying to move an important matter forward.

That experience shaped a direct and practical service model: listen carefully, identify the actual task, clarify what can and cannot be done, organize the information, and maintain documented follow-through.

Michael works directly with clients, attorneys, businesses, property owners, agencies, and other professionals to coordinate assignments and maintain clear communication from intake through completion.

Our Core Values

Reliability

We accept assignments carefully and work to complete approved tasks according to the confirmed instructions and scope.

Communication

Clients should know what was accepted, what is happening, what information is missing, and what the next step will be.

Organization

Important work requires clear records, consistent naming, document control, accurate dates, and organized files.

Respect

Every client deserves professional treatment regardless of background, circumstance, income, case type, or complexity.

Transparency

Scope, pricing, responsibilities, limitations, and expected deliverables should be discussed before work begins.

Follow-Through

An assignment is not complete merely because an attempt was made. Results, problems, and next steps must also be communicated.

Scope-First Service

Paper Chasers LLC uses a scope-first approach because different clients may use the same words to describe very different needs.

Before accepting an assignment, we work to clarify:

  • What specific task must be completed;
  • What documents or information are available;
  • What deadline or time pressure exists;
  • Who is responsible for decisions and approvals;
  • What result or deliverable is expected;
  • What limitations may affect completion; and
  • What pricing and payment terms apply.

Defined Assignment

We identify the work being requested instead of relying on vague assumptions or open-ended expectations.

Defined Responsibility

Clients remain responsible for factual accuracy, legal decisions, required signatures, deadlines, and final approval.

Defined Deliverables

The expected documents, reports, attempts, deliveries, or other completion materials are identified whenever possible.
