Process Service Built on Professional Standards

When legal documents need to be served, professionalism matters. Timing, accuracy, documentation, discretion, and clear communication can affect how smoothly a matter moves forward. Paper Chasers LLC approaches process service as part of a broader commitment to professional administrative and litigation support.

We assist clients with service of legal documents, notices, court papers, and related materials where permitted by law. Each assignment begins with a clear review of scope, location, timing, available information, and documentation needs before service attempts begin.

Our operating standard is simple: confirm the scope, confirm the timeline, perform the assignment professionally, and provide documented follow-through when appropriate.

Important: Paper Chasers LLC is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice or legal representation. We provide process service, administrative support, litigation support, courier support, and document-related services where permitted by applicable law.

How the Process Works

1. Submit the Request

Use the request page or contact us directly with the document type, service address, deadline, and any special instructions.

2. Confirm Scope & Pricing

We review the assignment, confirm availability, discuss timing, and confirm pricing before work begins.

3. Service Attempts Begin

We conduct service attempts based on the confirmed scope, location, schedule, and information provided.

4. Updates When Appropriate

We provide meaningful updates when appropriate, especially for urgent, difficult, or time-sensitive assignments.

5. Documentation

When service is completed or further action is needed, we provide appropriate documentation based on the assignment.

6. Next Steps

If additional attempts, alternate addresses, courier support, or related administrative assistance is needed, we discuss those options.
