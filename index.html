About

Paper Chasers LLC provides practical, professional support for individuals, small businesses, and legal professionals. We operate with a documented workflow: confirm scope, confirm timeline, execute, and deliver.

Community: Paper Chasers LLC is a proud sponsor of The People’s Paralegal.

NOTICE (NO LEGAL ADVICE): Paper Chasers LLC is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. Any legal research or document support is administrative in nature and performed only where permitted by applicable law. If legal advice is required, consult a licensed attorney.

Services

  • Legal research and document support (administrative support; no legal advice)
  • Process service (service of documents as permitted by law and local rules)
  • Courier and transport services (pickup, delivery, and time-sensitive runs)
  • General professional support services (project-based assistance)

How It Works

  1. Request: Contact us with the task, timeframe, and any relevant details.
  2. Confirm: We confirm scope, timeline, and pricing before work begins (email/text acceptable).
  3. Deliver: We complete the work and provide the agreed deliverables and documentation.

Scope & Terms

Services are provided on a project-based or task-based basis, with scope defined in advance whenever possible. Work is limited to the services expressly agreed upon between Paper Chasers LLC and the client.

Requests falling outside the original scope will be identified before additional work is performed. No expanded scope will be assumed without confirmation.

Fees, timelines, and deliverables may vary depending on the nature and urgency of the request. Payment terms are communicated prior to or at the time services are rendered unless otherwise agreed.

Client information is handled with discretion. Reasonable care is taken to maintain confidentiality; however, disclosure may occur where required by law or court order.

LIMITATION OF LIABILITY: To the fullest extent permitted by law, Paper Chasers LLC’s liability for any claim arising from services provided shall be limited to the amount paid for the specific service giving rise to the claim.
