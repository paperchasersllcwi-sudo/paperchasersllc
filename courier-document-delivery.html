Reliable Delivery for Important Documents

Important documents often need more than ordinary mailing. Attorneys, businesses, property managers, individuals, agencies, and organizations may need a document picked up, delivered, filed, returned, or transferred within a specific time.

Paper Chasers LLC provides professional courier and document delivery services with clear instructions, direct communication, status updates, and delivery confirmation when available.

Each assignment is reviewed for pickup location, destination, deadline, document type, handling instructions, recipient requirements, and expected proof of completion.

Our approach: Confirm the route, verify the instructions, protect the materials, complete the delivery, and document the result.

For Attorneys and Law Firms

Courthouse runs, filing delivery, document exchanges, client pickups, opposing-counsel delivery, and litigation-material transport.

For Businesses and Organizations

Contracts, records, notices, applications, checks, packets, signed documents, and other time-sensitive business materials.

For Individuals

Personal document pickup and delivery when dependable, documented transport is needed.

Courier and document delivery are different from legal process service. Documents requiring formal service must be accepted and handled as a process-service assignment.

What We Need From You

  • Pickup address and contact person;
  • Delivery address and recipient information;
  • Required delivery date and time;
  • Description of the documents or materials;
  • Number and approximate size of packages;
  • Any special handling instructions;
  • Required signatures, receipts, or stamped copies;
  • Applicable filing or processing fees;
  • Office hours and known access restrictions;
  • Return-delivery instructions;
  • Parking, security, or building-entry information;
  • Client or attorney contact for questions;
  • Instructions if the office or recipient is unavailable; and
  • Any confidentiality or chain-of-custody requirements.
Documents should be complete, properly labeled, and ready for transport before pickup. Paper Chasers LLC may decline loose, unsecured, improperly packaged, unsafe, unlawful, or unidentified materials.

Important Service Limitations

No Automatic Acceptance

Submitting a request does not mean the assignment has been accepted. Availability and scope must be confirmed.

No Filing Guarantee

We cannot guarantee that an office will accept, process, stamp, file, or approve any document presented for delivery.

No Legal Review

We do not review documents for legal sufficiency or determine whether they comply with court rules or legal requirements.

No Deadline Calculation

Clients and attorneys are responsible for calculating and communicating all legal, contractual, and administrative deadlines.

No Prohibited Materials

We do not transport illegal goods, hazardous substances, weapons, controlled substances, cash without prior approval, or materials requiring licensing we do not possess.

No Guaranteed Access

Office closures, security procedures, restricted access, weather, traffic, recipient unavailability, and other conditions may affect delivery.

Rush, after-hours, long-distance, multi-stop, return-trip, waiting-time, filing-fee, parking, toll, and special-handling charges may apply. Pricing is confirmed before the assignment begins whenever reasonably possible.
