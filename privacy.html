Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Paper Chasers LLC collects, uses, and protects information submitted through our website. We aim to collect only what is necessary to communicate with you and perform requested services.

Plain-English Summary: We use your information to respond to your request, confirm scope/pricing, and complete the work you asked for. We do not sell your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you contact us or submit a request:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number).
  • Request details (task description, location, timeframe, and any notes you provide).
  • Attachments or documents you voluntarily provide by email, link, or other methods.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and service requests.
  • To confirm scope, pricing, scheduling, and deliverables.
  • To perform and deliver requested services.
  • To maintain basic business records (e.g., invoices, confirmations, and communications).

How Requests Are Delivered

Website service requests may be delivered to us through third-party form delivery tools that transmit your submission securely over HTTPS. Once delivered, your request is received and handled through our business communication channels.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in limited circumstances, including:

  • With service providers necessary to operate our business (e.g., payment processors and communication tools).
  • When required by law, court order, or valid legal process.
  • To protect rights, safety, and prevent fraud or misuse.

Payments

Payments are processed through Square or other approved payment processors. We do not store full payment card details on this website. Payment processors may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies and terms.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted to us. However, no method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be 100% secure. Please avoid submitting highly sensitive personal data through website forms unless requested and necessary for the work.

Retention

We retain communications and service records for as long as reasonably necessary for business, legal, and accounting purposes.

Your Choices

  • You may request to update or correct your contact information.
  • You may request deletion of information where appropriate, subject to legal/accounting requirements.
