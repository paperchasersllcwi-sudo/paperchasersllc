Documents Commonly Served

Depending on the assignment and applicable law, process service may involve the delivery of legal paperwork such as summonses, complaints, small claims documents, eviction-related papers, subpoenas, notices, motions, orders, restraining order paperwork, family court documents, business-related documents, and other materials that require documented delivery.
